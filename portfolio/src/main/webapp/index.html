Places

The Gym

Weightlifting is one of my hobbies; therefore, the gym is one of my favorite places to go to. I enjoy working out and pushing myself to improve each time I go to the gym. Although sometimes I may lack the motivation, I always come out of the gym feeling good knowing that I had accomplished something that will inevitably improve my health and well-being. Going with my friends motivates me a lot as we can push each other and hold each other accountable. When I put 250 pounds on my back and perform squats, I feel powerful and motivated to succeed in other endeavors. Weightlifting truly is one the coolest things that I used to do. Now that the coronavirus has basically cut off any access that I may have to a gym, given my remote location, it has unfortunately been a couple months since the last time I lifted. I sorely miss it.

Duke University

Duke University's campus is one of my favorite places on Earth. Duke feels like a completely different place, as if it is in its own bubble. The architecture is majestic and truly breathtaking. The facilities are packed with resources: I have access to over 40 local restaurants on campus alone, two enormous gym facilities, countless places to study, multiple libraries, about a dozen graduate and professional schools, and world-class professors. Although the overwhelming amount of resources available to is a huge draw, I feel like the people is what makes Duke such a great place. I've made so many amazing friends throughout my two years there and I have met so many valuable connections along the way. It's like home away from home.
